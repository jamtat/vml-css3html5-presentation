As the UK and Ireland's biggest hotel chain, we're delighted to continue our international expansion. Our hotels in the Southeast Asia region, due to open from 2014, offering a high quality service, a warm welcome and clean, comfortable rooms. Premier Inn offers a unique combination of excellent facilities at affordable prices. We guarantee you everything you need for a truly memorable stay.

Premier Inn will be opening hotels in major hubs in Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia and Singapore. All our Southeast Asia hotels are conveniently located so you can make the most of your stay for business or leisure, giving easy access to key business areas and tourist attractions.

At the end of a long day, you can either sit down to a delicious meal or unwind with a cocktail in the hotel's bar. Premier Inn is committed, putting the guest at the heart of everything we do.

Continuing to expand Internationally

We are excited by the opportunity to develop the Premier Inn brand outside of the UK. Premier Inn is the UK's favourite hotel company, consistently achieving the top ranking in consumer brand preference score and winning many awards. We believe Premier Inn is a highly transferable brand that will be equally welcomed and valued by international customers.

Over the past 6 years we have established a presence in two markets - the Arabian gulf and South Asia where we have a number of operational hotels and several under development. In 2014, we will commence operations in Indonesia, shortly followed by properties in Thailand, Singapore and Malaysia.
